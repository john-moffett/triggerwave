Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 17, 2026

Triggerwave ("we", "us", "our") operates the mobile game Tile Shatter (the "Game"). This page describes what data is collected when you use the Game and how it is used.

1. Data We Collect

We do not collect personal information such as your name, email address, or phone number. The Game does not require an account or login.

The following data may be collected automatically through third-party services integrated into the Game:

  • Device information — device model, operating system version, screen resolution, language setting.
  • Game analytics — gameplay events such as scores, levels reached, session length, and feature usage. This data is anonymous and used to improve game balance and player experience.
  • Advertising data — ad interactions (impressions, clicks). The advertising provider may collect a mobile advertising identifier (IDFA/GAID) to serve relevant ads, subject to your device-level privacy settings.

2. Third-Party Services

The Game uses the following third-party services, each with their own privacy policies:

  • GameAnalytics — analytics and A/B testing. Privacy Policy
  • Google AdMob — advertising. Privacy Policy
  • Google Play / Apple App Store — in-app purchases and app distribution. Governed by the respective platform privacy policies.

3. Advertising

The Game displays interstitial advertisements between gameplay sessions. You may purchase a "Remove Ads" option within the Game to permanently disable interstitial ads. Rewarded ads (which you choose to watch in exchange for in-game hints) are not affected by this purchase.

4. In-App Purchases

The Game offers optional in-app purchases processed entirely by the platform store (Google Play or Apple App Store). We do not receive or store any payment information.

5. Children's Privacy

The Game is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal data through the Game, please contact us and we will delete it.

6. Data Retention

Analytics data is retained by GameAnalytics according to their data retention policy. We do not maintain a separate database of user data. All game progress is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to our servers.

7. Your Rights

You can reset your advertising identifier or opt out of personalized ads through your device settings:

  • Android: Settings > Google > Ads > Reset advertising ID / Opt out of Ads Personalization
  • iOS: Settings > Privacy & Security > Tracking

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. Continued use of the Game after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
